Digital Service Design

Welcome to our project hub! We're a team committed to tackling the City of Helsinki's challenge of enhancing physical activity and overall well-being for upper secondary students through a thoughtful digital service. Focusing on essential aspects like sleep, nutrition, and physical activity, we're on a mission to weave technology seamlessly into the fabric of healthy lifestyles.

In the context of the Digital Service Design course from Haaga-Helia University of Applied Science, we're following a practical and insightful approach outlined in "Sprint: How to Solve Big Problems and Test New Ideas in Just Five Days" by Jake Knapp, John Zeratsky, and Braden Kowitz from Google Ventures (GV). This course guides us through a methodical process, helping us shape, test, and refine our digital service within a short timeframe. Together, let's explore the potential of technology in fostering healthier habits for the next generation.
