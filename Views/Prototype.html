Prototype

At this stage we started building a digital prototype foor the concept. We aimed for lower fidelity at first and planned to amp up the fidelity after user evaluations.

Tools used for this stage: Pen & paper Figma

Figma

To create the prototype of our service, we chose Figma. Here are some of the reasons behind this choice:
  • Industry Standard
  • Real Time Collaboration
  • Known by all members
  • Used in other projects by some members

Views

Our service seemed most fitted for a mobile application. Here are the main views for it:

Discovery

To help users build healthy habits, it is primordial to provide knowledge. The discovery views let the user find articles, videos, and recipes to help them in their habit-forging journey.

Challenges and Goals

Goals and Challenges form the base of our service. Goals are short-term objectives, like "Go for a 2km run", whereas challenges are more long-term objectives. "Run a total of 30km" could be a Challenge, and every time you accomplish a related Goal, it will make you progress on your Challenge. In this view, you can create new goals and challenges and also join existing challenges.

Tracker

To keep track of your progress, you can use the Tracker view. There you will find all your current Goals and Challenges, and their status. This view is also used to edit Challenges and set Goals as completed.

Users Feed

Another important part of our solution is the social aspect. Users are able to share their completed goals as posts in a manner similar to what you would find on the most well-known social medias.

Login

It is important that users have an account so that their progress can be saved and accessed from different platforms.
