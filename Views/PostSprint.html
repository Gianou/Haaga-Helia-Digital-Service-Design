Post Sprint

This was the time to analyse the results of our testing. Since the designing process that we are using is based on iteration, we then also improved our prototype to solve the issues that were pointed out during testing.

Tools used for this stage: Figma

Results of testing

From our testing session we discovered that some improvements could help to make our app more intuitive. The following issues came up during testing :
  • The difference between Goals and Challenge was not always clear
  • The steps to post in you feed were a bit confusing

Second iteration of the prototype

Based on this, we addapted our prototype :
  • Challenges/goals: colour and icons were added to the page to differenciate challenges and goals from each other
  • Challenges/goals: the layout of the page was simplified
  • Tracker: Colours were added for challenges and goals
  • Feed: Instructions were added to the feed
The prototype can be tested on Figma
